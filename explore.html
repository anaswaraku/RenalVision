Explore About Kidney Diseases

Learn about symptoms, causes, prevention, and more

Symptoms

Common symptoms of kidney diseases include fatigue, swelling, changes in urination, and more.

Causes

Kidney diseases can be caused by diabetes, high blood pressure, and other chronic conditions.

Prevention

Prevent kidney diseases by maintaining a healthy diet, regular exercise, and controlling blood pressure.

Treatment

Treatment options include medication, dialysis, and kidney transplantation depending on the severity.

Diet

A kidney-friendly diet includes low sodium, potassium, and phosphorus foods. Hydration is key.

Lifestyle

Adopting a healthy lifestyle can help manage and prevent kidney diseases. Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol.
